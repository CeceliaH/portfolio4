Title: Bodies and Buildings

Medium: Rhino

Description: My portfolio from ARCH124A, an architecture softwareclass I took summer 2018. We used Photoshop, Illustrator, and Rhino to relate bodies and buildings to each other creating different architectural drawings (section, plan, axonometric, oblique). The pose I chose was a female flipping through the air and the building case study was of the Farnsworth House.
